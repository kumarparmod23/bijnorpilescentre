  <h2>Who We Are</h2>
  <p>Bijnor Piles Centre is a specialised proctology clinic in <strong>Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh, India</strong>. We focus exclusively on anorectal disorders, so every patient receives focused, experienced care.</p>

  <h2>Conditions We Treat</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Piles (Bawasir / Hemorrhoids)</strong> — bleeding, swelling and pain during bowel movements</li>
    <li><strong>Anal Fissure</strong> — sharp pain and bleeding from a tear in the anal lining</li>
    <li><strong>Anal Fistula (Bhagandar)</strong> — recurring abscess and pus discharge</li>
    <li><strong>Pilonidal Sinus</strong> — painful cyst near the tailbone</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Treatments Offered</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Kshar Sutra</strong> — gold-standard Ayurvedic medicated thread therapy for fistula and pilonidal sinus</li>
    <li><strong>Kshar Karma</strong> — painless chemical cauterisation for early piles</li>
    <li><strong>Rubber Band Ligation</strong> — modern 10-minute day-care procedure for internal piles</li>
  </ul>
