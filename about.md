# About Bijnor Piles Centre

Bijnor Piles Centre is a dedicated proctology clinic in **Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh, India**,
specialising in the treatment of:

- **Piles (Bawasir)**
- **Anal Fissure**
- **Anal Fistula (Bhagandar)**
- **Pilonidal Sinus**

## Treatments Offered
- **Kshar Sutra** — ancient Ayurvedic para-surgical technique for fistula
- **Kshar Karma** — chemical cauterisation for piles
- **Rubber Band Ligation** — modern day-care procedure for internal piles

## Why Patients Choose Us
- Painless, day-care procedures
- Decades of clinical experience
- High success rates with minimum recurrence
- Personalised pre- and post-treatment care
